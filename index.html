About Evergreen Public School

Evergreen Public School is a Senior Secondary Co-educational English medium school affiliated to CBSE. Over the years the school has come to symbolize dedication to maintaining excellent standards of education, with consistent achievements in academics and extracurricular activities.

Principal: Manju Sharma, M.A., M.Ed — with 15 years administrative and teaching experience.

Our Facilities

Vision

Evergreen Public School aims to be an institution of excellence where sound ethical values, character, wisdom, leadership, service and academic achievement are encouraged in a safe and nurturing environment.

Mission

To provide an environment that emphasizes intellectual and emotional growth so every student discovers and realizes their full potential, with emphasis on integrity, ethics, responsibility and self-discipline.
